CST304

Publication Design 
Introduces page layout design for print and screen. Students work in Adobe InDesign and Adobe Acrobat. Emphasizes use of type, typesetting techniques, legibility, and design composition. Covers scanning, importing files, and mastering digital prepress production. Includes course related field trips.

The semester long course had students designing an original tri-fold brochure for CSUMB's "Study at Sea" program. The brochure needed to showcase what the learning environment was like as well as the adventures the students will be engaged in.
