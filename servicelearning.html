CST 461

Service Learning 

A project-driven Service Learning course in which advanced Communication Design students analyze and solve a design or technical problem for their community partners and their constituents. Students address social injustice issues and combine design and technical skills with professional knowledge to solve the problem. Requirement: concurrent enrollment in a 400-level Communication Design course.

Service Learning is a course taken in conjunction with the Web Production course, CST 451, to not only give students some experience in a work environment, but also a taste of what it is like to use one's abilities and skill sets to give back to the community to make a long term impact. The majority of the volunteer hours are subtracted from the work done during the class period, because we are creating a website for an actual non-profit organization as a client, with the exception of 20 hours to be completed outside of class. The best was I felt I could assist the organization was to help make the tutorial videos on how to use their drupal-built CMS website, as well as an active tech support for any questions that may arise.
